About Me

My Name is Jeremy Townsend, I am a musician at heart. I love to sing and I hope to use my love for singing to support me and my family. I was a music major for 3 years at ENMU, after some events transpired I decided to move here to Las Cruces with my parents and try to find a job. I landed a job at a call center, after 2 weeks I already hated that job.I decided I should come up with a plan to keep myself fed as I try to pursue becoming a professional musician. I ended up in IT because I thought it would be a good fit and have the best opportunities for me to do remote work. Ultimatly I want to sing around the world with some of the best professional choirs in the world.

Why I am taking this class

I am taking this class so that I can recieve my undergraduate degree and also to use the skills I have learned in this class to develope professional website for othe aspiring musicians such as myself. With the knowledge I gain from this class I hope to find a decent job that can support me.
